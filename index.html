Title: Autism Spectrum Visualization

Visualization of Autism data using Tableau to encode features of data in order to gain insights and answers about autism diagnosis..

Intrduction

Autism spectrum data set consists of important information about considered cases like their age, result, AQ score which is based on set of 10 questions asked to subjects,ethnicity, country among others.

Tableau was used to encode visualizations to the selected data-set to gain insights and answer few questions about the data.

We try to figure out some patterns in this dataset by asking important questions around their age,ethnicity and location. Answers to these questions provide insights about where autism cases are maximum, how its diagnosis is related to age and what role ethnicity plays, if any, in autism which we further drill down to country distribution.

QUESTION #1

Where is the highest proportion of autism diagnosis around the world?

ANSWER

It was observed that maximum number of cases were seen in the United States.It was observed that 53 out of 180 positive cases which amounts to 29.44% cases were seen in United States alone.

PROCESS

We use map encoding which uses color coding to explain density of autism patients in different parts of the world.

We make use of geographical data field- Country of residence too get geographical location and then we make use color encoding on field Result with a filter of those cases which had positive diagnosis

QUESTION #2

Does the Age affect diagnosis of autism?

ANSWER

Thus, as age of patient increases, diagnosis of autism becomes more conclusive while the slope of trend line for negative cases is relatively less

PROCESS

We try to find if age has a role to play in diagnosis of autism. In order to get an answer for this we design a scatter plot visualization along with trend lines of positive & negative cases

We take the median scores and average ages of subjects. Result indicates the screening scores for test takers with 1 being the least and 10 being most likely to have autism.

QUESTION #3

What is the role of ethnicity and country on autism?

ANSWER

Thus, we can say that most cases of autism are seen in white europeans which is about 60.56% and when we drill down to see ethnicity combined with country, highest cases are seen in United States (19.44%)

PROCESS

We try to understand what role does ethnicity play in autism. We further drill down to see how is this further affected by countries where these patients live.

We make use of following tree maps visualization to answer this question. As we can see, white Europeans have the highest percentage of autism cases and furthermore white europeans in United States have shown significantly larger cases of autism of about 19.44%
